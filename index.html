Welcome to Love in Haight Fairy Garden

A community art project bringing a little magic to the corner of Masonic & Page—one succulent at a time.

Plan your visit Support the garden

What is this place?

The Love in Haight Fairy Garden is a sidewalk succulent installation created entirely by hand—built brick by brick, planter by planter. Many arrangements live in upcycled teacups, jars, and found containers.

It’s run as a neighborhood nonprofit project to beautify the area, spark joy, and invite play. You’re welcome to explore respectfully, take photos, and share.

Quick facts

  • Location: Masonic & Page, San Francisco
  • Open: 24/7 sidewalk installation
  • Follow: @love_in_haight
  • Pay-what-you-can plant stand on fair-weather days

Visit
